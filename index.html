Welcome to Shmethan: The Mystical Audio Sequencer!


  • Click on the purple squares to create different drum and synth patterns
  • Click on the green triangles on the left to demo a sound
  • Click on the big green circle on the right (or press space) to start or stop playing your beat
  • Use the sliders on the right to change tempo or volume, or even add a little magic sauce
  • Feel free to edit your pattern while it's playing!
  • When you're happy with your beat, click on the red circle to record, click it again to stop recording, and click on the download icon to download

Enjoy!
